Alis Global

Alis Global marine consultancy & Recruitment services is a global workforce solutions provider servicing the needs of clients and contractors worldwide. Experts in several specialist recruitment areas we are able to source rare skills in the niche sectors. Whether you’re looking for a new job or a new employee, Alis Global are committed to delivering an unrivalled range of recruitment services covering permanent, contract, and interim appointments.

We provide experts across an array of specialist sectors including Construction, Aviation, Medical, Public sector, Oil & gas, Rail, Power & Energy, Accountancy, Finance and IT. We strive to maintain the highest standards while exceeding client’s expectations at all levels.
